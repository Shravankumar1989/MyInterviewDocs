Firewall Management (UFW)
-------------------------
    1. ufw status: Check firewall status.
    2. ufw enable: Enable firewall.
    3. ufw disable: Disable firewall.
    4. ufw allow <port>: Allow traffic on a specific port.
    5. ufw deny <port>: Block traffic on a specific port.
