Process Management
------------------
    1. ps aux: View running processes.
    2. kill <PID>: Terminate a process by its PID.
    3. killall <processname>: Terminate all instances of a process by name.
    4. bg: Resume a suspended job in the background.
    5. fg: Bring a background job to the foreground.
